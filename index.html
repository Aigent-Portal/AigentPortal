Frequently Asked Questions

What are webhooks?

Webhooks are automated messages sent from apps when something happens. Aigent Portal sends your inputs (text, files, triggers) to a unique URL you provide, which your AI agent or automation listens to.

Is Aigent Portal secure?

Yes. We don't require API keys, collect zero user data, and store all configuration locally on your device. Communication is direct between your app and your webhook URL. Header authentication is supported for endpoint security.

What kind of AI agents or automations can I connect?

Any system that can listen and respond to incoming webhooks! This includes custom AI models, platforms like n8n or Make, cloud functions, or your own backend applications.

Do I need my own server?

You need a publicly accessible webhook URL for your AI agent/automation. This could be self-hosted, a cloud function, or provided by an automation platform.

What data does the app store?

Only the configuration data you enter: webhook URLs and optional header authentication details. This is stored locally on your device and never sent to us.

When is the iOS version coming?

We are actively working on the iOS version and plan to release it soon! Stay tuned.

The only information the App stores is the configuration data you provide directly within the App:
